HoneyDAO is an investment collective founded by devs, influencers, investors and founders who have joined forces to provide value in the form of capital investment, partnership, social, community, and commercial support.

HoneyDAO is unique in its approach by allowing all members regardless of size to secure allocation while giving an equal vote to each member, representing a truly fair yet powerful approach we call the hive-effect.

Established in April 2021 with a fair-launch distribution, quickly attracting 100 genesis members. The DAO will soon open up the Hive to more member-bees with the goal of being the premier fair-venture-DAO in DeFi.
